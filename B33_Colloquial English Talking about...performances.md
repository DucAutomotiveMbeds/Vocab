# bouncing sth off sb
+ If you bounce something off someone, you tell someone about an idea or plan in order to find out what they think of it.

# bewildered(adj):  /bɪˈwɪl.dəd/
+ confused and uncertain
Example: I felt kinda bewildered about her response to me last night.

# intriguing(adj):  /ɪnˈtriː.ɡɪŋ/
+ very interesting because of being unusual and mysterious.
